Flat Tire Assistance

Don't let a flat tire ruin your day, Triangle Towing will replace your flat with the spare tire your vehicle has and you get back on the road in no time. If you do not have a spare we will tow your vehicle to your desired location.

Jump Starts

Did you forget to turn off your headlights? Is your car not starting? Your car battery could be dead. Triangle Towing will come to your location, inspect your vehicle and if the battery is dead we will jump start your car and get you back on the road.

Vehicle Lockouts

Occasionally we lock our keys in our car - It's embarassing and It's frustrating to not be able to get in your car. Triangle Towing is here to help - we are fully equipped to unlock locked vehicles safely and without damaging them. We will get you back on the road quickly and efficiently.

Refueling

Are you stuck on the side of the road with a car that has no gas? Perhaps your gas guage stopped working and you didn't notice you were driving to Charlotte on 1/4 tank. In any event, we have you covered. Triangle Towing will arrive on scene, get you enough gas to get back on the road and on your way.
