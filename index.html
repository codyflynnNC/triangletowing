Licensed and Insured

TriangleTowing ensures the safety of you and your vehicle by complying with all license requirements within the state of NC. All of our trucks are insured so you can rest easy knowing your vehicle is safe.

We Love Raleigh

TriangleTowing loves to engage with the community and support local business. We work with closely with police and emergency services daily to better serve our community. We also partner with local automotive repair shops and dealerships to provide our customers with mechanics who are trusted.

TriangleTowing understands not all tow jobs are created equal. We are prepared for any type of tow, such as: light towing of small vehicles, medium towing of large vehicles like tractors and oversized trucks and heavy duty towing such as semi's and trailers.

Accident Recovery
