What To Do If You Have Been In An Accident

Your safety is the number one priority. If you are injured please move to the side of the road and dial 911. If it is safe to do so, you can also move your vehicle to the side of the road, if not, turn on your hazards and get somewhere safe. Next, check others involved for injuries and dial 911 if needed. Be sure to gather info, take pictures and call the police once everybody is safe. Finally, contact us for a tow if needed and rest easy knowing you are getting the best tow truck service in Raleigh.
