5 Tow Truck Facts You Should Know

By TriangleTowing

Posted on August 24, 2013 at 9:00 PM

  1. Tow trucks come in all shapes and sizes. Contrary to popular belief there is more than one type of tow truck, for example, you have an integrated tow truck, flat-bed(our bread and butter), the wheel-lift, the boom and the hook and chain. Choosing the right tow truck for the job is of utmost importance and can make or break a company in this business.

  2. They say neccessity is the true mother of invention and the phrase couldn't be more true in the case of Ernest holmes. 100 years ago, Ernest was trying to retrieve a vehicle from a creek using rope and sheer man power. The group of men were struggling and then he had an 'Aha!' moment. He decided to attach a flat surface behind his truck, put it underneath the vehicle and then use that surface to pull the stuck car out, thus the tow truck was born. Thanks Ernest!

  3. Tow trucks have an entire musuem dedicated to them. Check it out at International Towing & Recovery Hall of Fame.

  4. Flatbeds are the weapon of choice in every towtruck companies arsenal, and for good reason. These types of trucks don't leave the wheels hanging during transportation and prevent damage to the transmission by keeping the vehicle stationary.

  5. Know the difference: A tow truck is a motorized truck used for moving wrecked, disabled, illegally parked or impounded vehicles. Not to be confused with a motor carrier - which is a car trailer primarily used to transfer passenger cars.
